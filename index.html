Hey there - welcome!
We're just a group of people who enjoy climbing. The Michigan Rock Climbing club (Mirocle) was first made in January, 2015 by a few Korean graduate students from the University of Michigan. Since then, it has quickly expanded to become a group that includes not only graduate students, but also undergrads, alums, and basically anyone who's interested in climbing with us!
Currently, we climb together as a group at a local gym called Planet Rock at the following days/times:
-Tuesday 7pm-10pm
-Thursday 7pm-10pm
-Saturday 3pm-6pm
We also like to go outdoor climbing whenever we can, so check out the "Memories" page to see where we've been around the country so far!
