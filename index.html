Michigan Rock Climbing (MIROCLE)

Hi there! We’re just a group of people who enjoy climbing.

Our club was first made in January, 2015 by a few Korean graduate students from the University of Michigan.
Since then, it’s gradually expanded into a larger crew that now consists of undergrads, graduate students, and alumni who work in the area.

Currently, we climb at a local gym called Planet Rock at the following days/times:
-Tuesday 7pm-10pm
-Thursday 7pm-10pm
-Saturday 3pm-6pm

We also like to go outdoor climbing whenever we can, so check out the “Memories” page to see where we’ve been around the country so far!

We always love to meet new people so don’t hesitate in contacting us if you’re interested in joining us for climbing or if you have any questions. Thank you.
